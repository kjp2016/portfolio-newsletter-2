AMZN

Amazon.com, Inc. (AMZN) has seen a month-to-date increase of $14.97 (7.87%). This uptick follows the company's announcement of a $14 billion investment in artificial intelligence (AI) to enhance its cloud computing services, aiming to compete with industry leaders like Microsoft. ([reuters.com](https://www.reuters.com/breakingviews/amazon-is-everything-plus-ai-store-2024-04-30/?utm_source=openai)) Additionally, Amazon reported a 17% rise in revenue from its cloud division, Amazon Web Services (AWS), driven by increased demand for AI solutions. ([reuters.com](https://www.reuters.com/technology/amazon-forecasts-quarterly-revenue-below-estimates-2024-04-30/?utm_source=openai)) However, the company projected second-quarter revenue below Wall Street expectations, citing higher capital expenditures for AI and AWS infrastructure. ([reuters.com](https://www.reuters.com/technology/amazon-forecasts-quarterly-revenue-below-estimates-2024-04-30/?utm_source=openai)) Looking ahead, Amazon's strategic focus on AI investments and cloud services positions it to capitalize on the growing demand for AI-driven solutions.

AVGO

Broadcom Inc. (AVGO) has seen its stock price increase by $35.31 (17.89%) month-to-date. This surge follows the completion of Broadcom's $69 billion acquisition of VMware, enhancing its multi-cloud offerings and transitioning VMware's business model to a subscription-based service, which is expected to boost average selling prices and revenue growth. ([nasdaq.com](https://www.nasdaq.com/articles/can-ongoing-rally-broadcom-avgo-stock-extend?utm_source=openai)) Additionally, Broadcom's partnership with Google Cloud aims to optimize VMware workloads, integrating advanced IT infrastructure and generative AI capabilities. ([investor.wedbush.com](https://investor.wedbush.com/wedbush/news/read/44061919/broadcom_?utm_source=openai)) The recent acquisition of Seagate's System-on-Chip operations for $600 million further strengthens Broadcom's SoC product portfolio. ([sec.gov](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1730168/000173016824000080/avgo-20240505.htm?utm_source=openai)) These strategic moves position Broadcom to capitalize on expanding cloud and AI markets, potentially sustaining its growth trajectory.
